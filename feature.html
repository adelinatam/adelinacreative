Project: CTC Website

As the Economic and Marketing Coordinator for Chinatown Transformation Collaborative Society of Edmonton (CTC), I worked with a team of web developers to create their website. As a graphic designer, my job included deciding the layout of the website, creating design elements, and choosing a color scheme that fits with our primary logo.

The creation process involved sketching the website's layout, prototyping using Adobe Illustrator and JustInMind and launching the website through Dreamweaver.

CTC's website promotes Chinatown as a destination spot, Chinatown's history, culture and the importance of the Chinese community in the growth of the City of Edmonton.
